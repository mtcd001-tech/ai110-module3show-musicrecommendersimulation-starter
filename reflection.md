Profile Comparison Reflections

Lofi vs. Metal Profiles: The Lofi profile receives much more variety in its Top 5 because the dataset is heavily skewed toward chill music. In contrast, the Metal profile effectively "locks" onto one song, showing that the recommender is only as good as the library it draws from.

The "Gym Hero" Problem: To a non-programmer, I would explain that "Gym Hero" keeps showing up for "Happy Pop" fans because my system gives a massive "bonus" just for being in the same category. Even if a song's energy is a bit too high, that +25 point genre boost acts like a "VIP pass," allowing it to skip to the front of the line ahead of better-fitting songs from other genres.

Impact of the Weight Shift: By doubling the importance of Energy, I noticed that "Coffee Shop Stories" (Jazz) started appearing for Lofi fans. This makes sense because even though the genre name is different, the "feeling" (energy level) is almost identical.
